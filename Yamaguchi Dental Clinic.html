The dentist

A quiet idea, kept for twenty years.

— save the natural tooth, wherever it can be saved.

Dr. Fumiyo Yamaguchi opened this practice near Bashamichi in 2006, after training in periodontics at the University of Pennsylvania and presenting at dental congresses across the United States. Her English was earned the same way her patients' trust is — slowly, in rooms where being understood matters.

The work here leans toward the conservative: treat the cause, preserve the structure, and reach for an implant or a crown only when the tooth truly cannot be kept. It is unhurried dentistry, explained plainly, in a language you are comfortable in.

“I'd rather save your tooth than replace it.” 「歯を残す」ことに、こだわります。

What we do

Treatments

General and specialist dental care under one roof — from a routine check-up to advanced gum therapy.

  • 01 General & preventive care Check-ups, fillings and the maintenance that keeps trouble away.
  • 02 Periodontal & gum therapy our specialty Specialist treatment for gum disease, including tissue regeneration.
  • 03 Root canal & nerve-preserving care Saving the tooth — and its living nerve — wherever possible.
  • 04 Implants For when a tooth genuinely can't be kept, restored with care.
  • 05 Ceramic & cosmetic dentistry Natural-looking ceramic crowns, inlays and minimal-prep work.
  • 06 Whitening A brighter, even shade — gently, on your timeline.
  • 07 Clear-aligner orthodontics Discreet alignment for adults, with removable aligners.
  • 08 Dentures & bridges Comfortable replacements, including tooth-sparing bridges.
  • 09 Wisdom-tooth removal Assessed carefully, removed only when it's the right call.
