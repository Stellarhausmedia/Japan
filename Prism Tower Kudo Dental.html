The practice

Healthy gums are the foundation of every lasting smile.

Prism Tower Kudo Dental is a periodontics-led clinic in Meguro — the kind of detail-obsessed care that protects teeth for decades, not just months. We treat the structure beneath the smile first, then build beauty on a foundation that holds.

For Tokyo's international residents, good treatment shouldn't get lost in translation. We explain every step in clear English, so you always understand your options, your costs and your care — before anything begins.
