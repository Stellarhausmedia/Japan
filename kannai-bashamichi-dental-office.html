IHeritage · 馬車道

Where modern Japan was first lit.

In 1872, the first gas lamps in the country flickered to life on Bashamichi — the moment Yokohama opened its doors and the West and Japan began to meet, here on this street.

A century and a half later, we keep that welcome lit: thoughtful, modern dentistry for everyone who calls Naka-ku home — in whatever language they speak best.
