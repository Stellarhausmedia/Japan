Treatments

Everything your
smile needs,
under one roof.

From your child's first check-up to full-arch implant restoration — comprehensive dental care in a calm, modern clinic two minutes from Kominatodori Station.

Implants

Permanent tooth replacement that looks, feels, and functions like your natural teeth. Single implants to full-arch restoration.

Orthodontics

Traditional braces and clear aligner options to straighten teeth at any age, with transparent progress reporting in English.

Whitening

Professional in-clinic whitening for visible results, with at-home maintenance kits to keep your smile bright between visits.

Cosmetic

Veneers, bonding, and smile design consultations to refine the aesthetics of your teeth, tailored to your face and goals.

Paediatric

Child-friendly care from first teeth through teenage years. Gentle technique, clear explanation, and a space that doesn't feel intimidating.

Oral Surgery

Extractions, wisdom teeth, and minor surgical procedures carried out with precision and full aftercare instructions you can actually understand.
