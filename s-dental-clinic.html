New to Japan? You're in good hands

Dentistry that speaks your language — on the bay.

Moving country shouldn't mean putting off the dentist. At S Dental Clinic Yokohama Minato Mirai, our team sees patients in English and walks you through every step — what's happening, what the options are, and what it costs — before anything begins.

You'll find us on the first floor of Yokohama Gate Tower, a one-minute walk from Shin-Takashima Station and an easy stroll from Yokohama Station. Evenings on weekdays, Saturdays too.

What we do
