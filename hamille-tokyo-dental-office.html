I  ·  Welcome

A quiet practice for careful teeth

SSet among the antiquarian bookshops of Jimbōchō and Ogawamachi, Hamille is a small dental office with an unfashionable habit: we take our time. Visits are unhurried, explanations are plain, and questions are always welcome — the manner of a good reading room, applied to your teeth.

Because our director studied and practised across Canada, Australia and the United States, English-speaking patients are genuinely at home here. You will not be hurried through a translation app or left guessing what was done. We talk things through, in English, until they make sense.

From routine cleanings to orthodontics, whitening and implant care, treatment is made to measure for the person in the chair — never off a conveyor belt. 丁寧 · teinei — careful, courteous, done with attention. The single word that best describes how we would like every visit to feel.

II  ·  Contents

Care, set out plainly

Check-ups, cleanings and the quiet maintenance that keeps small problems small — cavities, gum care and honest advice on what does, and doesn't, need doing.

Clear-aligner and conventional orthodontics for adults and children. Our director is a certified Invisalign Platinum Provider. Mostly private treatment.

Replacing missing teeth with implants, planned carefully and, where needed, in step with university-hospital specialists. Private treatment.

Natural-looking restorations and whitening — in the chair or at home — aimed at a tooth and gum colour that still looks like you. Private treatment.

Wisdom-tooth removal, troublesome canker sores and minor oral surgery — gently handled, with hospital referral when a case calls for it.

Calm, patient first visits for younger patients — building the kind of early experience that makes the dentist an ordinary place to be.

Also kept on the shelf: sports mouthguards, anti-snoring appliances, jaw-joint (TMJ) care and treatment for persistent bad breath.

III  ·  From the director

A short note before you visit

I trained as a dentist in Japan and abroad, and over many years one belief has only grown firmer: good dentistry is mostly good explanation.

If you have ever left a clinic unsure what was actually done — or stopped going because the chair frightened you — I would like to change that. We explain things in plain language, base every recommendation on evidence rather than pressure, and treat each patient with the courtesy due a guest.

Please come and say hello. I think you will find dentistry can feel quite different here.

Gen Udagawa Dr. Gen Udagawa · Director, Hamille
